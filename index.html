Our Story

Step into the heart of Ranchi and discover the story behind Cookery. Founded in 1964, our culinary adventure began with a simple idea: to craft extraordinary dishes that reflect our commitment to quality, innovation, and a love for flavors that dance on the palate. From the rustic charm of our decor to the artistry in our kitchen, every element at Cookery is a testament to our dedication to providing an exceptional dining experience. We source the finest, locally-sourced ingredients to ensure that each dish tells a story of authenticity and freshness.
